Pakistan Mein RAW Ke 3 Bade Agents Giraftaar: 30 April Latest Reports

Published: April 30, 2026 | New Delhi Bureau

NEW DELHI: Aaj subah Pakistan ke Karachi aur Lahore shehron mein bade paimane par intelligence operation ke baad dawa kiya gaya hai ke Bharat ki RAW agency se talluq rakne wale 3 dehshatgardan ko giraftaar kiya gaya hai. Is khabar ne poore khitte mein sansani phella di hai.

Sources ke mutabiq, in agents ke paas se hassas dastawezat aur modern communication equipment baramad hue hain. Halanki Bharat ki wizarat-e-kharija ne abhi tak is par koi rasmi bayan jari nahi kiya hai, lekin social media par inki tasaveer viral ho rahi hain. Kaha ja raha hai ke ye log pichle 2 saal se ek bade mission par kaam kar rahe the.

Pakistan ke dakhli sheheron mein security high alert par hai. Reports ke mutabiq, ye giraftariyan tab amal mein aayin jab ek agent ne border ke qareeb encryption signals ka istemal kiya jo ke intelligence agencies ne trace kar liya tha. Mazeed tehqiqat jari hain aur umeed hai ke jald hi bade khulase honge.

Yahan ye batana zaroori hai ke 30 April ki ye report ab tak ki sabse badi update maani ja rahi hai. Analysts ka kehna hai ke is waqiye se dono mulkon ke talluqat mein mazeed talkhi paida ho sakti hai. Mazeed maloomat ke liye hamare sath jude rahein.
