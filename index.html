Pakistan Mein RAW Ke 3 Bade Agents Giraftaar: 30 April Ki Sabse Badi Khabar

Published: April 30, 2026 | Reported by: Amit Sharma, New Delhi

NEW DELHI: Aaj subah Pakistan ke Karachi aur Lahore shehron mein bade paimane par intelligence operation ke baad dawa kiya gaya hai ke Bharat ki RAW agency se talluq rakhne wale 3 agents ko giraftaar kiya gaya hai. Is khabar ne poore dunya mein sansani phella di hai.

Sources ke mutabiq, in agents ke paas se hassas dastawezat aur modern encryption devices baramad hui hain. Pakistan ki intelligence agencies ka dawa hai ke ye network pichle do saal se ek gupt mission par kaam kar raha tha. Bharat ki wizarat-e-kharija (MEA) ne ab tak is par koi rasmi rad-e-amal zahir nahi kiya hai.

Expert analysts ka kehna hai ke 30 April ki ye report aane wale dino mein dono mulkon ke talluqat mein mazeed kashidgi paida kar sakti hai. Security forces ne border areas mein high alert jari kar diya hai.

Tehqiqat jari hai aur mazeed khulase hone ki umeed hai. Hamare saath jude rahein mazeed tafseelat ke liye.
